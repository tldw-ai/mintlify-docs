# No-code Playground

The **No-code Playground** is a sandbox environment designed to let you quickly experiment and experience the full capabilities of our technology—no coding required. Dive into interactive examples, test powerful video search capabilities, and explore intuitive ways to integrate TL;DW into your workflow.

• **Easy exploration:** Browse and interact with example collections.

• **Instant results:** See our AI-powered video search in action.

• **Hands-on testing:** Experiment directly, no technical setup needed.

# Get started with playground&#x20;

1. [Sign up](https://app.trytldw.ai/auth/signin) for our playground to get started.

2. **Navigate to the** [**Examples**](https://app.trytldw.ai/examples) **page**, and select an example collection.

3. **Enter your search query** into the search bar to quickly find exact moments within the video.

4. **Review results on the video timeline**: segments matching your query are highlighted—the darker the color, the better the match.

5. **Click the video title** to open the detailed view, where you can explore enriched metadata: short descriptions summarizing matched events and tags sorted by frequency and relevance.
